Who We Are

Built on people, not just presses

Michigan Shippers Supply has been family-owned and operated for 57 years. Over that time the tools have changed, we print on flexographic and modern digital presses now, and still on metal, but what hasn't changed is how we treat the people we work for.

We're a small business that's spent decades helping other small businesses and startups get off the ground. Our in-house design team helps you build out your brand, and we're small enough to care about your very first run while experienced enough to handle your biggest order.
