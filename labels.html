How It Works

From idea to printed labels in three easy steps

No design experience needed. Whether you have print-ready art or just a rough idea, our team makes it simple.

1

Share your idea or files

Tell us what you're making and send any artwork you have, or nothing at all. We accept Illustrator-friendly files like .AI, .EPS, .PDF, and .SVG.

2

We design & send a proof

Our in-house team builds out your label and sends a proof for your approval. Nothing goes to press until you love it.

3

We print & ship, fast

Once approved, we print, finish, and ship your order, with most jobs out the door in 10 days or less.

📝 Every order is proofed for your approval before we print, and we stand behind every roll that leaves our shop.

Still printing on foil products

Keurig-compatible foil lids, on the cups you brew

It's exactly the kind of work we love: helping family businesses and fast-growing brands get their label onto every single cup. From Keurig-compatible foil lids to durable metal and tin labels, if you can dream it up, our team will help you make it.

Questions & Answers

Frequently asked questions

Not seeing your question? Email sales@michiganshippers.com, a real person will help.

How fast is your turnaround?
Most orders ship in 10 days or less, the same fast turnaround for small first runs and large production orders alike. Have a tight deadline? Ask us; we'll do our best to help.
Is there a minimum order?
We love helping startups and small businesses, so we keep things friendly for first runs and small batches. Tell us what you need and we'll size the job to you.
What file formats should I send?
Vector files are best, .AI, .EPS, .PDF, and .SVG open straight in Adobe Illustrator and stay crisp at any size. High-resolution .PNG or .TIFF also work. No artwork yet? Our in-house design team will build it for you.
Do I get a proof before you print?
Yes. We send a proof for your approval before anything goes to press, so you know exactly what you're getting.
What can you print on?
Paper and film label stocks in a range of finishes, plus metal and foil, including Keurig-compatible coffee-pod lids and durable tin labels that most printers won't touch.
Where are you, and where do you ship?
We're a family-owned shop in Spring Lake, Michigan, serving Michigan, Indiana, Ohio, and Illinois, and shipping beyond.
Can you hold inventory for me?
Yes, ask about our managed-inventory and just-in-time printing program. Keep just a day's or a week's supply on hand and we'll produce the rest as you need it. Available with our premium packages.
